About Miraila

Miraila is a basic public chain project initiated by the Miraila Foundation, with a unique DEPOS+PBFT consensus and DAG distributed storage. Positioning the next-generation commercial-grade basic public blockchain, which aims to build a trust network of value circulation,and is committed to creating a distributed cross-chain business ecosystem with extensive digital trust, free value circulation,and mass shared applications. Miraila is characterized by multi-chain, multi-language, high concurrency, citation abstraction and complete ecology.
